# MINERS-SALES-DASHBOARD
An interactive executive dashboard built to analyze overall sales performance, cost metrics, product category distribution, global revenue geography, and monthly sales trends.
## Dashboard Overview
![Miners Sales Dashboard](MINERS%20SALES%20DASHBOARD.png)

## Key Metrics & KPIs Summary

| Metric | Value | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Total Sales** | **$16.6M** | Overall gross revenue generated |
| **Total COGS** | **$13.9M** | Total Cost of Goods Sold |
| **Total Profit** | **$2.7M** | Net profit realized across all sales |
| **Units Sold** | **153.1K** | Volume of physical product units sold |
| **Profit Margin** | **30%** | Overall profit efficiency percentage |

## Key Insights & Visual Breakdown

### 1. Monthly Revenue Trends (`Total Sales by Month`)
* **Peak Revenue Months:** October (**$2.7M**), December (**$2.6M**), and May (**$2.5M**) represent the strongest sales spikes of the year.
* **Low Revenue Months:** March (**$0.1M**) and April recorded the lowest sales figures, indicating seasonal slumps or inventory restocks.
* **Q4 Momentum:** Strong performance in Q4 (Oct–Dec) drives significant baseline profitability.

### 2. Product Category Performance (`Total Sales & Gross Sales by Product`)
* **Laptops** dominate as the primary revenue driver at **$16.65M** (100% baseline).
* **Keyboards** (**$16.21M**) and **Monitors** (**$15.87M**) closely follow, forming the core high-margin technology stack.
* **Routers** and **Printers** round out sales at **$14.76M** (88.65%) and **$15.18M** (91.18%), respectively.

### 3. Geographic Revenue Distribution (`Total Sales by Country`)
* Global distribution map highlights key regional markets across North America, Europe, and South America, allowing dynamic filtering by target territories

## Interactive Features & Slicers

* **Product Selector Dropdown:** Allows granular drill-downs into individual hardware products (Laptop, Keyboard, Monitor, Smartphone, Printer, Router).
* **Quarterly Slicers (`Qtr 1` - `Qtr 4`):** Quick toggle filters to evaluate quarterly seasonality and performance benchmarks.

## Data Architecture & Tech Stack

* **BI / Visualization Tool:** Power BI / Excel / Tableau *(customize as applicable)*
* **Data Transformations:** Data cleaning, custom column calculations, and DAX / SQL queries for dynamic metric generation.
* **Data Model:** Star schema structure linking Fact Sales tables with Product, Geography, and Date dimensions.
